fn preferred_irq(&self) -> Option<(PciInterruptPin, u32)>

Preferred IRQ for this device. The device may request a specific pin and IRQ number by returning a non-None value. Otherwise, an appropriate IRQ will be allocated automatically. The device’s assign_irq function will be called with its assigned IRQ either way.

Assign a legacy PCI IRQ to this device. The device may write to irq_evt to trigger an interrupt. When irq_resample_evt is signaled, the device should re-assert irq_evt if necessary.
